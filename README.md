# Urban Air Quality Analysis Dashboard (2015–2024)

## Understanding Urban Air Pollution Through Data

Air pollution is one of the most serious environmental challenges faced by modern cities. Rapid urbanization, increasing vehicle usage, and industrial activities have significantly impacted air quality across many regions.

However, understanding pollution patterns is not easy. Raw air quality datasets contain thousands of records collected across multiple cities and time periods. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify trends, patterns, or the key pollutants contributing to poor air quality.

This project aims to answer an important question:

**What can air quality data tell us about pollution trends in major Indian cities?**

To answer this, an interactive Power BI dashboard was built to explore air pollution data from **2015 to 2024** across several major cities.

---

# The Questions This Dashboard Answers

The dashboard is designed to guide users through a series of analytical questions.

---

# 1️⃣ What Is the Overall Air Quality Trend?

The **Urban Air Quality Overview Dashboard** provides a high-level understanding of pollution levels.

It answers questions such as:

• What is the **average air quality index (AQI)** across cities?  
• What are the **maximum and minimum AQI values recorded**?  
• How has air quality **changed over time from 2015 to 2024**?  
• Are there **seasonal patterns** in pollution levels?  
• How are AQI values distributed across **air quality categories** such as Good, Moderate, Poor, and Severe?

Through these insights, users can quickly understand whether air quality is improving or worsening over time.

---

# 2️⃣ Which Cities Experience Higher Pollution Levels?

The **City-Level Analysis Dashboard** focuses on comparing pollution levels across cities.

This section answers questions like:

• Which cities have the **highest average AQI levels**?  
• How does **monthly AQI vary across cities**?  
• Do certain cities experience **strong seasonal pollution spikes**?  
• How does the distribution of **AQI categories differ between cities**?

By analyzing these patterns, users can identify cities that consistently experience higher pollution levels and observe how pollution changes throughout the year.

---

# 3️⃣ Which Pollutants Contribute the Most to Air Pollution?

The **Pollutant Contribution Analysis Dashboard** explores the role of individual pollutants.

It answers questions such as:

• Which pollutants have the **highest average concentration levels**?  
• How do pollutants like **PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, and CO** change over time?  
• Which pollutants contribute the **largest share of air pollution**?  
• Is there a relationship between **pollutant levels and overall AQI**?

This analysis helps highlight which pollutants play the biggest role in worsening air quality.

---

# Key Insights From the Analysis

From the dashboards, several important observations can be made:

• **PM2.5 is one of the largest contributors to poor air quality.**  
• Some cities consistently experience **higher AQI levels compared to others.**  
• Pollution levels often show **seasonal patterns**, with certain months experiencing higher AQI values.  
• There is a **strong relationship between particulate matter levels and overall AQI.**

These insights demonstrate how data visualization can help reveal meaningful patterns in environmental data.
